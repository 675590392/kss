Detainees Notice

All detainees' activities in the detention house must obey  and other rules and regulations, as well as police discipline.

  1、Prior to entering or exiting the cell, Be prepared to receive both physical and personal belongings checks. You must be cooperative during the time an officer is carrying out the checks. 
  2、Prior to entering the cell and after exiting the cell, you must immediately squat down facing the wall beside the door. You can stand up only  if permitted by an officer, otherwise, squat down facing the wall.
  3、If you were standing at the time  when an officer is opening the cell door, you should immediately squat down. 
  4、Handcuffs must be put on prior to exiting the cell 
  5、Prior to receiving a conversation from a police officer, you must stand in front of the officer's door and verbally report to the police, then, enter with permission.
  6、In police investegations or lawyer meetings, it's prohibited to carry or hide letters/documents on you without permissions. If you need to bring letters/documents, ask for police permissions. The same rule applies when retuning to the cell after meetings.
  7、During the first visit by your consulate staff, Let them know that you need some RMB to be deposited to your account so you can purchase some living necessities 
  8、If you refuse to meet your consulate staff or lawyer, you must file in a written report with your signature on.
  9、Do not cross over the red line inside the cell.  Do not make any loud noises in the cell. 
  10、You can not talk to people in other cells. This behaviour severely violates the regulation 
  11、During exercises in the cell, be ware of your body movement radius, do not interfere other's regular activities. Do not practice any moves endanger yourself or others.
  12、When you are not feeling well, report to police by pressing the alarm bell on the wall or report to police directly. 
  13、Detainees can file in a relief request letter provided by the detention house to have living nessisities and money sent by families. The monthly allowance for sending money is 2000 RMB. Upon receiving the goods. Please sign to confirm all items. 
  14、The address of Shanghai Detention House: 1760 HuNan Road, Shanghai Pudong New Area. Zip: 201204
  15、Detainees are allowed to buy nessisities and food twice per month, the allowance each time is 200RMB and the monthly total is no more than 400RMB(except holidays with special permissions); upon receiving the goods, please confirm all items with your order, if any problem with quantity or quality, report to police immediately to have the goods exchanged or made up for by the supplier.
