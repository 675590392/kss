Rights and Obligations of Detainees in ShangHai Detention House

                           
(Penitentiary Administration Department of Shanghai Municipal Public Security Bureau)

一、Detainees shall enjoy the following rights:

  1.1 Upon detention to receive medical check-up and medical attention and treatment of illnesses during the period of detention.
  1.2 Lawful property belonging to the Detainee shall not be illegally confiscated. Such property under the custodial care of the Detention House shall in cases of damage or loss be compensated accordingly save in Acts of God or such circumstances outside the control of the Detention House.
	Daily necessities purchased in the Detention House shall not exceed the local retail price and such purchases shall be acknowledged in writing by the Detainee.
  1.3 Have sufficient food and adequate drinking water in accordance with the set standards and sleeping time of not less than 8 hours per day; and some outdoor activity per day except in situation of rain, snow or other unexpected weather condition.
  1.4 Be accorded due respect and not suffer any physical abuse, insult, bully, torture or any corporal punishment by any prison warden or other cell mates. Do not take orders form other inmates.
  1.5 Right to request the transfer of documents including appeal, prosecution, charges and appointment of legal counsel to the relevant departments without being retained or delayed or destroyed by the Detention House.
  1.6 Under legal circumstances, right to correspond and meet family members, lawyer or other defence counsel.
  1.7 Where voting rights are not abolished or political rights deprived, the right to vote is protected.
  1.8 Minority groups and foreign detainees to be accorded due respect for their beliefs, practices and culture.
  1.9 Female detainees shall be supervised by female prison wardens. Female detainees, minors and those with medical problems shall receive the appropriate care.
  1.10 Detainees serving sentence who qualify for remission shall receive such reduction or conditional release.
  1.11 Where rights are infringed or violated, the right to prosecute and the right of complaint.
  1.12 Continue to enjoy the rights conferred upon by other regulations and directives and receive such other legal assistances where applicable.


二、Detainees shall have the following Obligations:

  2.1 Observe and comply all relevant laws and provisions and the Regulations of the Detention House and to follow the instructions of the prison wardens and the armed police on duty.
  2.2 Strictly adhere to the set standards for daily activities and carry out such work or rest activities as scheduled and receive such legal，cultural, moral and other education.
  2.3 Upon discovery of other inmates' attempt or plan or execution to escape, commit crime or suicide, to report the incident immediately and take such steps to prevent or stop such activities. Detainees shall not with hold such information or protect any persons involved.
  2.4 Not to form any gang, bully, physically abuse, punish, torture or insult other inmates or be involved in any affray.
  2.5 Not to hide or possess any knife, weapons, sharp articles, rope, cigarettes, matchsticks, lighters or similar prohibited articles.
  2.6 Maintain good and clean personal hygiene and cell cleanliness. Graffiti and carvings on the wall is strictly prohibited.
  2.7 Protect public properties; do not vandalise such public facilities, equipment and properties belonging to the Detention House.
  2.8 Not to forcibly possess and use property belonging to the other inmates. Save as permitted by the prison warden, not to lend or offer as gifts or use any clothings, food, property or money to the other inmates.
  2.9 Not to snatch food or increase one's allotted share; not to share food and drink.
  2.10 Not to change allotted sleeping position without permission; not to share blanket; not to cover and hide one's head in the blanket when sleeping.
  2.11 Not to communicate with inmates from other cells or pass written messages or goods; not to request or assist other inmates to pass oral or written messages or otherwise.
  2.12 Report sickness on first opportunity. Be truthful with sickness, and co-operate with treatment. Not to hide medicine, reject treatment, cause physical harm or abuse oneself, go on hunger strike or feign illness.
  2.13 Comply with other regulations or orders of the Detention House.


三、Detainees Right to Assistance

  3.1 Directly report to the prison warden or use the alarm button inside the cell to make the report.
  3.2 Make appointment to meet the Chief Prison Warden (or the Director of the Detention House)
  3.3 Reflect the situation to the In-House Prosecution Officer or make appointment to meet him through the prison warden.
  3.4 Make a written representation to the relevant legal authorities.
  3.5 Reflect the situation through appointed legal counsel or family members to the relevant department.
  3.6 Reflect the situation to invited visiting officials or inspectors.
  3.7 Using additional available reporting channels as provided for under other relevant directives or rules.
