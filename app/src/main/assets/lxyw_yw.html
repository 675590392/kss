1 Observe and comply all relevant laws and provisions and the Regulations of the Detention House and to follow the instructions of the prison wardens and the armed police on duty.
2 Strictly adhere to the set standards for daily activities and carry out such work or rest activities as scheduled and receive such legal，cultural, moral and other education.
3 Upon discovery of other inmates' attempt or plan or execution to escape, commit crime or suicide, to report the incident immediately and take such steps to prevent or stop such activities. Detainees shall not with hold such information or protect any persons involved.
4 Not to form any gang, bully, physically abuse, punish, torture or insult other inmates or be involved in any affray.
5 Not to hide or possess any knife, weapons, sharp articles, rope, cigarettes, matchsticks, lighters or similar prohibited articles.
6 Maintain good and clean personal hygiene and cell cleanliness. Graffiti and carvings on the wall is strictly prohibited.
7 Protect public properties; do not vandalise such public facilities, equipment and properties belonging to the Detention House.
8 Not to forcibly possess and use property belonging to the other inmates. Save as permitted by the prison warden, not to lend or offer as gifts or use any clothings, food, property or money to the other inmates.
9 Not to snatch food or increase one's allotted share; not to share food and drink.
10 Not to change allotted sleeping position without permission; not to share blanket; not to cover and hide one's head in the blanket when sleeping.
11 Not to communicate with inmates from other cells or pass written messages or goods; not to request or assist other inmates to pass oral or written messages or otherwise.
12 Report sickness on first opportunity. Be truthful with sickness, and co-operate with treatment. Not to hide medicine, reject treatment, cause physical harm or abuse oneself, go on hunger strike or feign illness.
13 Comply with other regulations or orders of the Detention House.
