Directions on the Alarm Equipment for Being Abused for the Suspects for Crime, Defendants and Criminals in Custody in the Detention House


In order to guarantee the legal rights of the suspects for crime, defendants and criminals in custody; ensure the safety of you and the detention house, you can use the alarm equipment under the circumstances below：

  1、Being assaulted or mistreated by others in the same cell.
  2、Having affrays in the cell.
  3、Finding suicide, attempted escape, assault, disturbances or bullying in the cell.
  4、Sudden occurrence of disease.
  5、Other emergencies.
  The police officers on duty must handle these as soon as possible.
