1 Upon detention to receive medical check-up and medical attention and treatment of illnesses during the period of detention.
2 Lawful property belonging to the Detainee shall not be illegally confiscated. Such property under the custodial care of the Detention House shall in cases of damage or loss be compensated accordingly save in Acts of God or such circumstances outside the control of the Detention House.Daily necessities purchased in the Detention House shall not exceed the local retail price and such purchases shall be acknowledged in writing by the Detainee.
3 Have sufficient food and adequate drinking water in accordance with the set standards and sleeping time of not less than 8 hours per day; and some outdoor activity per day except in situation of rain, snow or other unexpected weather condition.
4 Be accorded due respect and not suffer any physical abuse, insult, bully, torture or any corporal punishment by any prison warden or other cell mates. Do not take orders form other inmates.
5 Right to request the transfer of documents including appeal, prosecution, charges and appointment of legal counsel to the relevant departments without being retained or delayed or destroyed by the Detention House.
6 Under legal circumstances, right to correspond and meet family members, lawyer or other defence counsel.
7 Where voting rights are not abolished or political rights deprived, the right to vote is protected.
8 Minority groups and foreign detainees to be accorded due respect for their beliefs, practices and culture.
9 Female detainees shall be supervised by female prison wardens. Female detainees, minors and those with medical problems shall receive the appropriate care.
10 Detainees serving sentence who qualify for remission shall receive such reduction or conditional release.
11 Where rights are infringed or violated, the right to prosecute and the right of complaint.
12 Continue to enjoy the rights conferred upon by other regulations and directives and receive such other legal assistances where applicable.
