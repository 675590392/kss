Detailed  Regulation Regarding Water Usage by the Detainees at Shanghai Detention House


In response to the national initiative on conserve energy resources,reduce pollution discharge and environmental protection, and to actively promote the awareness of environmental conservation such as the positive effect of conserve water resources and the disgraceful effect of water wastage , the following is the regulation on water usage,each detainee should therefore follow it accordingly. 

  1、During washing,should control the water flow,keep it at low to moderate volume.In between brushing teeth,washing hands, washing clothes and wipe or soap your body,should turn off the water tap on a timely manner, to ensure the good practice of“Turn it On to use,and Off when it’s done!”;
  2、As to the cleaning of the toilet/washing area,do a moderate cleaning daily,and a major thorough cleaning once a week. Do try to conserve the water by wipe the area clean;
  3、When use the toilet,stop flushing the water once the toilet is clean. Do not keep the water continuously flushing while using the toilet;
  4、Leftover water from washing clothes and blankets can be recycled for washing the toilet or the washing area;
  5、When notice any leakage or over flowing of the water tap,Please report to the officer for repair on a timely manner. When notice any water wastage behaviour,please do remind each other and stop such behaviour mmediately.
