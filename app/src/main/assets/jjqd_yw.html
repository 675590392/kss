1 Directly report to the prison warden or use the alarm button inside the cell to make the report.
2 Make appointment to meet the Chief Prison Warden (or the Director of the Detention House)
3 Reflect the situation to the In-House Prosecution Officer or make appointment to meet him through the prison warden.
4 Make a written representation to the relevant legal authorities.
5 Reflect the situation through appointed legal counsel or family members to the relevant department.
6 Reflect the situation to invited visiting officials or inspectors.
7 Using additional available reporting channels as provided for under other relevant directives or rules.
